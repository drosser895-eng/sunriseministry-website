Welcome to Sunrise Ministry

I'm Rev. James D. Rosser, and it's my honor to guide couples through one of life's most beautiful milestones. At Sunrise Ministry, we believe in the profound power of love and commitment, and we are dedicated to crafting wedding ceremonies that are as unique and meaningful as your own love story.

We celebrate love across all denominations and walks of life, ensuring every ceremony is filled with joy, respect, and spiritual guidance. Your journey together begins here, with a ceremony that truly reflects who you are.

Our Wedding Officiant Services

Whether you envision an intimate elopement or a grand celebration, Sunrise Ministry offers personalized services to make your wedding day unforgettable.

  • **Custom-Crafted Ceremonies:** Tailored to your unique story, values, and traditions.
  • **Spiritual Guidance:** Thoughtful and inclusive spiritual elements woven into your ceremony.
  • **Vow Assistance:** Support in writing personal and heartfelt vows.
  • **Rehearsal Coordination:** Ensuring a smooth and confident walk down the aisle.
  • **Legal Filing:** Handling all necessary paperwork with care and precision.

We are committed to creating a ceremony that resonates deeply with you and your loved ones.

Your Love, Our Mission

Every wedding we officiate is a testament to the enduring power of love. We are here to serve, to celebrate, and to strengthen the bonds within our community. Through each ceremony, we aim to inspire hope, foster connection, and remind everyone that love always prevails.
